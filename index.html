UI/UX Design

COGS187A || CookNook WebApplication

Interaction Design || Collaboration

CookNook was a team project for COGS 187A: Usability & Information Architecture. The web application was targeted towards college students to take the contents of their pantry and fridge, then guide them to make a dish that would maximize the usage of ingredients. The highlight of the project a dynamic recipe search that would refresh each time an ingredient was added to the users’ virtual pantry.

CookNook was created by Team Git Rekt. The team walks through the project process via an in-depth series of Medium posts on the project. You can find them here!
